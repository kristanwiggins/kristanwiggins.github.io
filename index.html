So, if you hadn't noticed from the large print at the top, my name is Kristan Wiggins. Here's a little about me:

  • I am a soon-to-be journalism alumna from the University of Florida College of Journalism and Communications. I've worked in newsrooms on and off-campus and am a copy editor to my core.

  • I worked for three years at the largest student-run newspaper in the country, The Independent Florida Alligator, working in positions from copy editor all the way up to editor-in-chief.

  • I will be heading to New York City this summer thanks to the Dow Jones News Fund as a copy editing intern for The New York Times working on the metro desk.

  • I am a lover of cats and trivia and a grammar nazi extraordinaire.

  • I am out of bullet points.

This site should be an estimation of what I’ve done and what I want to do — but knowing me, that list will keep growing by the day. Stay tuned.
